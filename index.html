About Us

Fab Metal Private Limited is a modern manufacturer specializing in LM series aluminium ingots, from LM-0 to LM-25. We focus on delivering consistent quality and precise specifications for every grade, supporting industries such as automotive, engineering, and casting.

LM Series Aluminium Ingots Specifications

  • LM-0: Commercially pure aluminium (min. 99.5% Al)
  • LM-2: Aluminium-Silicon-Copper alloy, good castability, used in automotive components
  • LM-4: Aluminium-Silicon alloy, pressure die casting, good fluidity and corrosion resistance
  • LM-5: Aluminium-Silicon alloy, higher silicon, used for intricate castings
  • LM-6: Aluminium-Silicon alloy, very high corrosion resistance, marine/chemical applications
  • LM-9: Aluminium-Silicon-Magnesium, high strength, used for pistons and cylinder heads
  • LM-13: Aluminium-Silicon-Nickel-Copper, excellent wear resistance, used in pistons
  • LM-14: Aluminium-Magnesium-Silicon, used in aircraft and high-strength applications
  • LM-16: Aluminium-Silicon-Magnesium, general engineering, good machinability
  • LM-20: Aluminium-Zinc-Magnesium-Copper, high strength, used in aerospace
  • LM-21: Aluminium-Magnesium, corrosion-resistant marine fittings
  • LM-24: Aluminium-Silicon-Copper, pressure die casting, widely used in automotive and hardware
  • LM-25: Aluminium-Silicon-Magnesium (AlSi7Mg), high strength, weldable, heat-treatable, used in structural castings
